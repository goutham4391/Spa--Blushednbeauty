PERMANENT JEWELRY

What is permanent jewelry?
The latest jewelry trend that is custom, fit to you and welded closed so it stays forever (or as long as you wish!) These light & dainty chains are long-lasting and do not tarnish, turn, or rust. Chains are clasp-less, painless, and hassle-free!

CURATED EAR

What is a Curated Ear?
A combination of jewelry pieces styled in different piercings throughout the ear.
